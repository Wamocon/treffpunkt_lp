Why TreffPunkt

Five clear advantages for groups who want to meet without the chaos.

No account for participants

Invite by link – participants click, respond and join. No download, no registration.

Everything in one place

Event, date, location, tasks and budget on one central overview page. No more 200 WhatsApp messages.

Clear task assignment

Who brings what? Who books where? Binding tasks with status and deadline instead of loose chat promises.

Transparent budget planning

Who owes what to whom? Automatic per-person calculation and a clear cost breakdown for all participants.
